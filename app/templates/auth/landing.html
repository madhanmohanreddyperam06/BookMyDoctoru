How It Works

System Configuration - Admin creates doctor profiles, assigns specialities, sets consultation modes, and manages the complete system configuration. The admin has full control over doctor availability, shift scheduling, and system settings.

Easy Appointment Booking - Patients browse doctors by speciality and consultation mode, view available shifts and time slots, select preferred time, and receive a unique token number. The booking system prevents double booking through transactional locking.

Professional Consultation - Doctors conduct consultations, update appointment status, add prescriptions with medicines and instructions, and provide secure video links for online sessions. Patients receive complete medical care with detailed follow-up information.
